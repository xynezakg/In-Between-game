Game Rules: In-Between (Acey Deucey)

Welcome to **In-Between**, a fast-paced game of odds, bets, and card values! The dealer leads the action, and your goal is to bet on the gap between two cards.

Core Mechanics

  1. At the start of each round, the dealer reveals two cards: **Card A (Low)** and **Card B (High)**.
  2. These cards are sorted automatically. For example, if the dealer draws a 10 and a 4, they are placed on the table with the 4 on the left and the 10 on the right.
  3. All players then take turns deciding to **place a bet** (from 10 chips up to their maximum balance) or **pass (fold)**. If you pass, you sit out this round without losing chips.
  4. Once all bets are locked, the dealer draws **Card C (The Third Card)** and places it in the center.

Winning & Losing

  • Win (1:1 Payout): If the numerical value of the Third Card is **strictly between** Card A and Card B.
    Example: Table has **4** and **10**. Third card is **7**. You win!
  • Loss: If the Third Card is **outside** the range of Card A and Card B, or **equal** to one of them.
    Example: Table has **4** and **10**. Third card is **2** (outside) or **4** (equal). You lose!
  • Adjacent Cards (Auto-Loss): If the dealer deals two adjacent cards (e.g., **7** and **8**), there are no integers in-between. Betting is disabled or results in an automatic loss. It is recommended to Pass.
  • Pair (Auto-Loss): If the dealer deals two cards of the same value (e.g., **Jack** and **Jack**), they form a pair. The dealer cannot deal a third card "in-between" them. Any active bets lose automatically. It is recommended to Pass.
  • Ace Value: Aces are always high in this game (value of 14, above King).

Controls

  • Use the **preset buttons** (10, 50, All-in, etc.) or the **slider** to set your bet.
  • Open the **Settings** panel to toggle sound, felt themes, card sizes, or adjust animation speeds.
